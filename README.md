# GEOV272 – Seismic Interpretation of the Northern North Sea

Seismic interpretation report completed as part of GEOV272 at the University of Bergen, co-authored with Liv Pham Nestaas.

## Overview
Structural and stratigraphic interpretation of the northern North Sea, covering the Øygarden Fault Complex, Horda Platform, and Viking Graben. The study uses nine 2D seismic lines and the GN1101 3D survey, tied to four wells.

## Key Topics
- Horizon picking and fault interpretation in 2D and 3D (Petrel)
- Westward-deepening rift geometry from Late Jurassic rifting
- Quaternary glacial features: beach ridges, Norwegian Channel Ice Stream deposits
- RMS amplitude and variance attribute maps of the Top Ness horizon
- Evidence for hydrocarbon fluid flow: pockmarks, flat spots, reverse polarity
